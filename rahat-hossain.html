Rahat Hossain

Founding Member and Program Manager

Rahat Hossain is one of the founding team members of Leaping Boundaries, being engaged with Leaping Boundaries since June 2013. Rahat started working with Leaping Boundaries as a volunteer and was assigned as Program Manager in December 2013. In this position, he was maintaining the operation and financial part of the organization, as well as building relationships with the Madrasahs. In 2016, Rahat left Leaping Boundaries to fully commit to his own startup, CriticaLink (2014-2018). From 2019, Rahat works with Ayat Education, providing training and capacity building for nurses and midwives, as well as running healthcare services.

For his contribution to solving social challenges and healthcare, he was awarded Queen's Young Leader 2017. Rahat recently completed his second master's degree in the UK as a Chevening scholar in 2019 and also is a State Alumni - Young Professional 2017.
