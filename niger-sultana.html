Niger Sultana

Program Manager, Psychosocial Support

Niger Sultana completed her Masters from the Department of Educational and Counselling Psychology (DECP), Dhaka University and has received certification from the DECP on Communication Skills for Psychosocial Supporters and numerous other trainings. Niger joined Leaping boundaries in 2015 as a Program Manager and has been a key contributor in many projects of LB that focused on psychosocial well-being. Her responsibilities included recruiting volunteers, training volunteers, monitoring their field work, follow up, solving problems that come out of the field and also collecting data for research. Niger is also working at the Teachers and Guidance Centre of the University of Dhaka as a counsellor. She is passionate about working with adolescents and strives to ensure their maximum social and emotional wellbeing.
