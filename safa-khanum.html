Safa Khanum

Associate - HR, Admin and Accounts

Safa Khanum has completed her BBA in Finance from BRAC University. She joined Leaping Boundaries in 2018 as Accounts & Admin Assistant under the project with “Management Systems International”. From November 2018, she is working as an Associate, Admin & Accounts. She has received training from +Acumen on “Financial Modelling of Nonprofit Earned Income Accelerator” under SPRING. In LB, she worked to synchronise among management, vendors and beneficiaries to successful completion of administrational activities, arrangement of trainings, workshops and events. Establishing liaison among employees and management regarding HR activities, she has worked to communicate throughout the Organisation's organogram. In terms of assisting in accounts and financial activities, she has contributed in operations and project budgeting, making financial reports and maintaining documentation as per Organisation policy. Apart from assisting on organisational - Admin, HR, Accounts and Financial activities, she has worked directly on curating particular segments of different projects.
