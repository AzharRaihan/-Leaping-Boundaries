Sadia Afrin

Director of Leadership and Innovation

Sadia Afrin is a conscious leader working in the education sector who dreams about making education accessible to everyone through the power of innovation, technology and authenticity. Having trained over 1000+ youths and 150+ educators over the course of 4 years, Sadia has experience in working with various organizations including FreeTheMind, Teach for Bangladesh, Leaping Boundaries, Sesame Workshop Bangladesh, HerStory Foundation, Alokito Shishu Foundation etc. She graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from BRAC University, and has a Post Graduate Diploma in Educational Leadership from Institute of Educational Development, BRAC University.

In Leaping Boundaries she developed tools for youth to become empowering leaders and storytellers in classrooms so that they can read to children and help them become more aware of their surrounding issues regarding gender, rights and alternative futures. Her passion for working for youth has led her to serve as the Director of Leadership and Innovation in Leaping Boundaries, supporting in the organization development while building the capacity of young university students as well as the adolescent madrasa girls by training them on English, Leadership, Life Skills and other Socio-Emotional Skills.
