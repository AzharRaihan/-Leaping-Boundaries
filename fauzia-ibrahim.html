Fauzia Ibrahim

Program Director

Fauzia Azimun Hussain Ibrahim has more than 6 years of experience working across a wide range of industry including, social development, IT and education sector. Having graduated in Economics and Social Science from BRAC University, she has managed projects funded by international donors and has accumulated years of experience in research and report writing. She also completed an MBA in Banking from Dhaka University and is currently working as a Director for Programs in Leaping Boundaries. She managed a number of capacity building projects and coordinated between different components, design activities and training, organize advocacy meetings between different stakeholders and project beneficiaries during her tenure at LB.
